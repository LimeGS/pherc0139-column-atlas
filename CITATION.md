# Citation and source data

This atlas is a presentation and review aid built from Vesuvius Challenge
public segment data. It is not an independent transcription or ink detector.

## Required source-data citation

For the CT scans, segment meshes, surface volumes and ink predictions used by
this repository, cite:

> Giorgio Angelotti, Stephen Parsons, Sean Johnson, Elian Rafael Dal Prà,
> Johannes Rudolph, Paul Tafforeau, Alessandro Mirone, Paul Henderson,
> Hendrik Schilling, Forrest McDonald, David Josey, Youssef Nader, C. Seth
> Parker, W. Brent Seales. *Vesuvius Challenge - CT Scans of Herculaneum
> Papyri*. Vesuvius Challenge.

The official data portal specifies CC BY-NC 4.0 unless an individual asset
states otherwise. Confirm the current dataset citation and license before any
new publication: <https://vesuviuschallenge.org/data>.

## Paper reference

The published PHerc 0139 title and phrase readings referenced by this atlas
come from:

> Angelotti, Giorgio, et al. (2026). *Complete virtual unwrapping and reading
> of a rolled Herculaneum papyrus*. arXiv:2606.29085.

The record was submitted to arXiv on 2026-06-27:
<https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29085>.
